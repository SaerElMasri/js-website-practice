Merge Sort Algorithm

Merge sort works on the principle of divide and conquer. Merge sort repeatedly breaks down an array into two equal sub-arrays until each sub-array consists of a single element. Finally, all those sub-arrays are merged such that the resultant array is sorted.

Palindrome

A palindrome is a word or phrase that is the same both backwards and forwards. This palindrome definition means that if one were to read a palindrome from right to left instead of from left to right, the resulting word would be the same in both directions.

Prime Number

A prime number is a whole number greater than 1 whose only factors are 1 and itself. A factor is a whole number that can be divided evenly into another number.
